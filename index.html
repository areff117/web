Clear structure

Pages and sections arranged around how academics actually work: publications, courses, grants, projects, calls for papers.

Clean front end

Hand written HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Static hosting on GitHub Pages. Simple to deploy, simple to back up.

Research informed

Copy and layout shaped by experience in teaching, research, and evaluation. Designed for readers who care about methods and results.
